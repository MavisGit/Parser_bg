This work is a parser for the site bundestag.de. The parser looks through the page of each member of parliament and collects information about him(total ~ 750 member at the time of script creation).
End result:
1) data/person_card - ndividual cards with contacts in .json format;
2) data/list_members - ist of url addresses in .json format for parsing pages;
3) data/members - general table in .csv format with information on all members of parliament.
